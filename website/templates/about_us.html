We are Data Deviants

About Us

We are the Data Deviants, a team of data enthusiasts who love to explore, analyze, and visualize data. We believe that data is useful to unlocking insights and solving problems. We are passionate about turning data into actionable intelligence.


Our Story
We started as a group of students who met at a data science bootcamp in March 2023. We were assigned as a team for a project, but completed that project as a group of friends.

Our Vision
Our collective vision is to become well-versed and fluent in a variety of data services. We hope to harness the power of data to influence data-driven decisions that improve the quality of life and the world at large, perhaps even catalyzing world peace.

Our Mission
Our mission is to successfully deploy our customized and interactive web app.

Our Work
We are proud of the work we have done across various class activities and homework assignments, in domains such as: Crowdfunding Healthcare Human Resources Education Finance Travel You can see some of our projects on our GitHub pages.
